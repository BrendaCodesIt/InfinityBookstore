About Infinity Bookstore

Our Story

Welcome to Infinity Bookstore, your trusted community bookstore located in the heart of Kangema Town, opposite Equity Bank. Founded by Brenda Kagure Mwangi, we have been dedicated to fostering a love for reading and learning within our community.

We believe that books have the power to transform lives, inspire imagination, and build stronger communities. That's why we carefully curate our collection, with a special focus on Kenyan and African literature, to serve readers of all ages and interests.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be more than just a bookstore – we aim to be a cultural hub where the Kangema community comes together to:

  • Discover new worlds through literature
  • Access quality educational materials
  • Support local literacy and learning initiatives
  • Foster a sense of community through shared love of reading
  • Provide personalized book recommendations and excellent customer service

What We Offer

Diverse Collection

From classic literature to contemporary bestsellers, academic texts to children's books – we have something for everyone.

Expert Recommendations

Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to help you find your next great read.

Community Focus

We're committed to supporting local education and promoting literacy in Kangema Town.

Affordable Prices

Quality books at prices that make reading accessible to everyone in our community.
